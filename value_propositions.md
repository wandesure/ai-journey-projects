# Value Propositions

## AI Document Intelligence Hub - Industry-Specific Value

---

### Legal

Going through voluminous documents in legal space is daunting and not without omission of facts, evidence and references - our AI tool is your personal assistant that finds exactly what you need without reading the entire epistle. Whether you're prepping for discovery, reviewing contracts for that merger deadline, or hunting down that one clause buried in a 200-page agreement, our tool pulls the relevant sections in seconds. No more billable hours lost to document review - just straight to the insights that win cases.

---

### Healthcare

For healthcare administrators drowning in HIPAA audits, policy updates, and compliance documentation, we get it - one missed requirement can mean serious consequences for your patients and your organization. Our AI tool reviews your policies against healthcare regulations instantly, catching gaps before auditors do. Stop spending nights worrying about whether your documentation is complete - let our tool be the extra set of eyes that never gets tired and never misses a detail.

---

### Telecom

For compliance teams at telecom companies who spend days manually reviewing policies against CRTC and PIPEDA requirements, our AI tool analyses any document instantly and flags gaps - so audits take hours not weeks! No more spreadsheet tracking, no more "I think we covered that" moments during regulatory reviews. Just upload your policies and get a clear picture of where you stand, what's missing, and exactly what needs fixing before the regulators come knocking.
