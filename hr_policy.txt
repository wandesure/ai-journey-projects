ACME Corporation HR Policy

Recruitment:
All job postings must be approved by HR before publishing.
Interviews must include at least two panel members.
Background checks are mandatory for all new hires.

Leave Policy:
Employees are entitled to 15 days annual leave per year.
Sick leave requires a medical certificate after 3 consecutive days.
Parental leave is 12 weeks for primary caregivers.

Performance Reviews:
All employees receive annual performance reviews.
Reviews must be completed by direct managers.
Performance improvement plans must be documented in writing.

Code of Conduct:
Employees must treat all colleagues with respect.
Harassment of any kind will result in immediate termination.
Conflicts of interest must be disclosed to HR immediately.
