ACME Corporation Security Policy

Password Requirements:
All employees must use passwords of at least 12 characters.
Passwords must contain uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers and special characters.
Passwords must be changed every 60 days.
Employees must never share passwords with anyone including managers.
Multi-factor authentication is mandatory for all remote access.

Data Classification:
All company data must be classified as Public, Internal, or Confidential.
Confidential data must be encrypted at rest and in transit.
Confidential data must not be stored on personal devices.

Incident Response:
All security incidents must be reported within 24 hours.
The security team must be notified immediately for any data breach.
Employees must not attempt to investigate incidents themselves.

Access Control:
Access to systems must follow the principle of least privilege.
User accounts must be reviewed every quarter.
Terminated employees must have access revoked within 24 hours.
